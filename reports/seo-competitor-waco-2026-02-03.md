## Common SEO Patterns Observed
- Exact-match and partial-match domains rank often ("Waco + Concrete + [Company/Contractor/Works]").
- Title tags are keyword-first and location-heavy.
- Aggressive CTAs: multiple click-to-call, free estimate forms above the fold.
- Long-form FAQ or educational content on some competitor sites.
- Large service page clusters with many similar pages per service.

## Opportunities for Concrete Works LLC
- Differentiation: Many competitors use generic copy and networked sites. Lean into real local credibility, project proof, and McLennan County specifics already in your content.
- Trust assets: Emphasize verifiable reviews, address, photo-rich project pages, and transparent warranty language.
- Content gaps: Create targeted pages for "concrete driveway cost Waco", "stamped concrete patterns Waco", and "foundation slab prep Waco" with local soil context.
- Local intent capture: Expand location landing pages with unique content and job-specific proof.
- Conversion: Keep phone and form CTAs visible, but differentiate with "what happens next" and response-time promises.
